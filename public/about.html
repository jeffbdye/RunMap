About the site

This application is intended to help you chart map out your run and determine the distance of your route. See the source at GitHub.

Storage notices

We use LocalStorage to improve the functionality of this site. See below for a listing of what's stored and why.
  • runmap-last_focus: The center of the map when you last interacted with it. Used to re-center the map for future visits to the site.
  • runmap-use_metric: Your preference to display the run's distance in metric or miles.
  • runmap-follow_roads: Your preference to have the newly added segments follow roads or draw as a straight line.
  • runmap-help_notice: Whether or not you've dismissed the storage notice.
  • runmap-map_style: The map style visual you prefer to use.
  • runmap-last_run: The last route you plotted.
